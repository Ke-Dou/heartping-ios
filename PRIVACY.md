# HeartPing Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 01/11/2026

## Introduction
HeartPing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the HeartPing mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Required for account creation and authentication
- **Display Name**: Your chosen name shown to friends
- **Username**: Your unique identifier for friend discovery
- **Profile Picture**: Optional image you upload

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns and feature interactions
- **Push Notification Tokens**: To send you notifications

## How We Use Your Information
- **Account Management**: Create and maintain your account
- **Core Functionality**: Enable sending love messages to friends
- **Push Notifications**: Send you notifications when friends send love
- **App Improvement**: Analyze usage to improve the app experience

## Information Sharing
We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your information for marketing purposes

We may share information only:
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal requirements
- To protect our rights and safety

## Data Storage and Security
- **Firebase Services**: We use Google Firebase for secure data storage
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Control**: Strict access controls protect your information
- **Regular Updates**: We regularly update security measures

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your personal information
- **Update**: Modify your profile information
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data
- **Withdraw Consent**: Stop using the app at any time

## Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

## Children's Privacy
HeartPing is suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## Third-Party Services
HeartPing uses:
- **Firebase Authentication**: For secure login
- **Firebase Firestore**: For data storage
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: For push notifications
- **Firebase Storage**: For profile pictures

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for Firebase services.

## International Users
Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or email.
